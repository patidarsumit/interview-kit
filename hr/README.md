# HR Interview Preparation

This folder contains generic HR preparation that works for most companies.

It is tailored for your profile:

- Senior Software Engineer
- 6.5+ years experience
- Angular and TypeScript specialist
- enterprise frontend applications
- performance, accessibility, testing, CI/CD
- mentoring and code review experience
- exposure to Node.js, React, GraphQL, AWS, Docker

## Best Positioning

```text
I am a Senior Software Engineer with 6.5+ years of experience, primarily focused on Angular and TypeScript. I have worked on enterprise web applications in hospitality and government domains, with strong experience in reusable components, performance optimization, accessibility, testing, CI/CD, and mentoring.
```

## HR Interview Goal

HR usually checks:

- communication
- stability
- salary expectation
- notice period
- reason for change
- confidence
- culture fit
- honesty
- career direction
- whether your story matches your resume

## Golden Rule

Do not sound desperate. Do not sound arrogant.

Best tone:

```text
confident + honest + professional + flexible
```
